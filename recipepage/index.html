How To Make A PB&J


Supplies:

  1. Bread (anything but white bread)
  2. Peanut Butter
  3. Jelly / Jam / Preserves
  4. Knife
  5. Hands

Step 1:

Take 2 slices of bread.
Step 2:

Open jelly jar and spread as much as desired on 1 face of 1 of the slices of bread.
Step 3:

Open peanut butter jar and spread a liberal amount on 1 face of the other slice of bread.
Step 4:

Stack slices of bread so that the peanut butter and jelly covered faces are touching.
Step 5:

Eat!
